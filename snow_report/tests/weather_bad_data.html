Synopsis

Synopsis:  .A weakening cold front is sweeping through from the west this morning. A mix of sunshine and clouds will be the weather of the day overall with dry conditions as well. With the frontal passage this morning, gusty SW wind of 25 to 35 km/h near mid mountain with higher gusts in the alpine of near 50 km/h or possibly higher will ease steadily by afternoon. High pressure and a very mild flow will take over on Friday with mostly sunny and dry weather.

Extended Outlook

Saturday to Monday:  A dry and mild SW flow will prevail on Saturday with some sunshine. A weak upper trough will slide through from the west late on Sunday with more cloud cover but only a chance of a little precipitation expected for the resort. A cooler NW flow Monday will follow, with more dry weather on the way. Freezing levels will soar Saturday to near 3000 m. Near 2000 m Sunday afternoon and 1400 m Monday. Springlike conditions!
